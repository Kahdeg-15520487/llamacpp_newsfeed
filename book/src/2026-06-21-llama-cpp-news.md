### [Model Support] — PR #24340: Support Step3.5/3.7 flash mtp3

**Merged:** 2026-06-21 | **Author:** @forforever73

**Summary:** Implements multi-layer MTP (Multi-Token Prediction) speculative decoding for Step 3.5 and Step 3.7 flash models, replacing the previous single-block approach. Each MTP head now has its own decoder layer and KV cache, and the driver chains them sequentially — one `llama_decode` per head per step — achieving up to ~15% higher token throughput on both DGX Spark (GB10) and Mac Studio M4 Max compared to the single-head loop. Benchmarks at `--spec-draft-n-max 2` show accept rate improving from ~65% to ~74% on DGX Spark (30.8 → 33.9 tok/s on code_python) and from ~64% to ~74% on Mac Studio (42.6 → 45.3 tok/s). The PR introduces `cparams.mtp_layer_offset` for selecting which MTP block the graph runs, and the loader now requires all `n_layer_nextn` MTP blocks.

**Affected areas:** speculative decoding, MTP, Step3.5, Step3.7, model support, performance
